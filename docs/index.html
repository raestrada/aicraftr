Current Projects in Development

StoryCraftr

StoryCraftr is our first tool in the AI Craftr suite, designed for writers creating novels and stories. It leverages OpenAI to assist in outlining, world-building, character development, and chapter generation.

PaperCraftr

PaperCraftr is our academic writing assistant, tailored for researchers and students. It helps structure academic papers, organize literature, manage citations, and refine arguments with AI assistance.

Future Tools to Supercharge Your Workflow

AI Craftr is not just a writing suite—it's a vision for a smarter, more efficient way to tackle complex tasks across various fields. Here’s a sneak peek at what’s to come:

AgileCraftr

Designed for project managers and teams, AgileCraftr will streamline planning, tracking, and collaborative workflows. It integrates AI for intelligent task management, risk analysis, and productivity insights, ensuring your projects stay on track and on time.

LegalCraftr

LegalCraftr is set to assist legal professionals in drafting, reviewing, and organizing legal documents. With smart templates, case reference tools, and AI-driven text analysis, you’ll have everything you need for clear, compliant, and efficient documentation.

TestCraftr

Ideal for educators and trainers, TestCraftr will simplify the creation of quizzes, exams, and learning assessments. With tools for automated question generation, grading, and feedback, you’ll be able to design interactive, tailored assessments for students at any level.

AI Craftr is committed to making powerful AI tools accessible across all professional fields, transforming the way you work and creating smarter, faster workflows. Stay tuned as we bring these tools to life!
